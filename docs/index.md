# llmstxt-social

llmstxt-social helps organisations become visible to AI assistants like ChatGPT and Claude. It automatically generates an `llms.txt` file — a standardised, machine-readable description of what your organisation does, who it serves, and how to get in touch.

Think of it as SEO for AI: just as search engine optimisation helps people find you on Google, llms.txt helps AI systems understand and accurately describe your work.

## Who is it for?

llmstxt-social is built for UK social sector organisations:

- **Charities and voluntary organisations** — help funders and the public discover your services through AI
- **Funders and foundations** — make your funding programmes findable by AI-powered grant searches
- **Local councils and public bodies** — document your services so AI assistants can direct residents to the right place
- **Social enterprises and startups** — present a clear, AI-readable profile to investors and partners

## How it works

1. **Enter your website URL** and select your organisation type
2. **We crawl your website** intelligently, reading your about page, services, contact details, and more
3. **AI analyses your content** to extract key information about your mission, services, and impact
4. **You get a ready-to-use llms.txt file** that you can upload to your website

With a paid generation, you also get:

- **Data enrichment** from official sources like the Charity Commission and 360Giving
- **A quality assessment** with scores, grades, and actionable recommendations
- **Gap analysis** showing what's missing from your website
